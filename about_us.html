INTRODUCTION AND BACKGROUND


We would like to introduce Potential Consultants Co. Ltd, a Management Consultancy and Research Firm.

We offer a wide range of services to clients various sectors of the economy.


ORGANIZATION AND STAFFING


The firm is registered as a professional consultancy. Its growth in recent times has been an all-time high because we have adopted a policy to admit into senior management seasoned-personnel with proven capabilities at local and international level.

The firm is divided into functional divisions in order to accommodate expertise in Management consultancy, Institutional and Organizational Development, Support Services to the Water Sector, Research and Management Training to ensure efficiency of services to our clients.


MISSION AND STRATEGY


Our mission is to deliver competitively priced, value added public sector service provision, consultancy and related services that respond to particular needs of our clients through superior capability, innovation and team approach. The client is paramount and the focus of our activities.

Our strategy is concentrated in all clients, mainly through industry knowledgeable representatives, as well as teams who specialize in respective industries within our client portfolio.
